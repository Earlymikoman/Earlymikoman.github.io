Excalibur Engine

Excalibur Engine is my homemade C++ engine I made over the summer to further my understanding of engine architecture. My primary goal with this engine was to be as efficient as possible; pure ECS, interrupt messaging, anything I could think of that would minimize unnecessary code as much as possible. For the most part this is now abandonware, but much of what I did here will definitely be carried over into my future projects, engine and no. One of the main general tools I learned while developing Excalibur was templates, which I now know that I love dearly.
