Noirish

Press the sideways green "E" to start. Press 'R' to reload your gun.


Noirish was my first real experience coding, before I went to school or anything, just opening Unity and trying to tutorial my way to something. The codebase isn't particularly impressive, really very very inefficient, inelegant, and just lacking all of what I would now call common sense. But, for all its technical flaws, I did wind up getting a working "demo" for it that, for the time, felt good enough to bolster my want to get into computer science.
